Welcome to the official GitHub organization of the **Cybersecurity Society at IIEST Shibpur**. We are a passionate, student-driven community dedicated to exploring the vast and dynamic world of information security, ethical hacking, and digital privacy.

Our mission is to foster a culture of cybersecurity awareness on campus, build a strong team of ethical hackers, and contribute to the global open-source security community.


### 🎯 Our Core Domains
We focus on a wide range of cybersecurity disciplines, training our members to tackle modern digital threats:
* **Offensive Security & Pentesting:** Identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities in networks and applications.
* **Capture The Flag (CTF):** Competing in global Jeopardy and Attack/Defense style CTF competitions.
* **Cryptography:** Breaking ciphers, understanding encryption algorithms, and securing data.
* **Reverse Engineering & Pwn:** Analyzing compiled binaries and exploiting memory corruption vulnerabilities.
* **Digital Forensics & OSINT:** Investigating cybercrimes, analyzing memory dumps, and gathering open-source intelligence.


### 🚀 What You'll Find Here
This GitHub organization serves as the central hub for our community's technical projects:
* **CTF Writeups:** Detailed solutions and methodologies for challenges we've cracked.
* **Custom Tools & Scripts:** Open-source security tools developed by our members.
* **Workshop Materials:** Slides, code snippets, and vulnerable labs used during our campus sessions.
* **Cheatsheets & Roadmaps:** Curated resources to help beginners start their cybersecurity journey.
